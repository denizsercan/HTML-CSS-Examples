Mathematician

John von Neumann was a Hungarian-American pure and applied
mathematician, physicist, inventor, and polymath. He made major
contributions to a number of fields, including mathematics, physics,
economics, computing and statistics.Wikipedia

Died: February 8, 1957, Washington DC. United States
